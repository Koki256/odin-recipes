Recipe for Spaghetti Carbonara

Recipe for 4 portions of beautiful Spaghetti Carbonara topped with fresh Herbs

This is just the right recipe if you're looking for some fresh, creamy, tasty, and incredibly beautiful Spaghetti Carbonara that everyone in the Family will enjoy

These are the ingredients you need:

  • 400 grams of uncooked Spaghetti
  • 200 grams of uncooked Bacon
  • 4 Eggyolks
  • 50 grams of unsalted Butter
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Pinch of Nutmeg
  • own preference of freshly grated Parmesan

In order to make these tasty Spaghetti Carbonara you need to follow these Steps:

  1. Cook the the pasta in heavily salted Water
  2. Cut the bacon into cubes and fry with a little bit of butter
  3. Mix the eggyolks, salt, pepper and nutmeg in a big bowl
  4. Stir the butter till creamy and add to the eggyolk mix
  5. Mix the parmesan and the bacon with the eggyolks
  6. Add the cooked Pasta to the bowl

Et Voila enjoy your freshly cooked Meal!
